Pita

Description

A delicious middle-eastern flatbread used to make shawrama and falafel sandwiches

Ingredients

  • flour 300g
  • yeast 4g
  • salt 4g
  • sugar 3g

Steps

  1. Add sugar,salt,yeast, and water into a large bowl
  2. Add flour until a shaggy dough forms
  3. Knead the dough for 5-10 mins
  4. Let it rest for an hour
  5. Preheat your oven at 245Â°c for 30 mins
  6. Cut the dough into 6 balls
  7. Let them rest for 15 mins
  8. Roll the balls into 6 inch circles
  9. Bake the circles one by one on the baking tray for 2-3 mins
