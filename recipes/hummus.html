Hummus

Description

A versatile healthy mediterranean dip made from chickpeas

Note : The recipe requires a Food Processor

Ingredients

  • chickpeas 3 cups
  • garlic cloves two
  • tahini 1/3 cup
  • olive oil
  • fresh lemon juice
  • salt

Steps

  1. Soak the chickpeas overnight
  2. Boil the chickpeas with a tsp of baking soda till the skin starts peeling
  3. Remove the skin by washing under the tap
  4. Blend the chickpeas in the food processor
  5. Add all the other ingredients and 6-7 ice cubes
  6. Blend for 2-3 minutes until smooth
  7. Take it out and Drizzle some olive oil
  8. Enjoy
