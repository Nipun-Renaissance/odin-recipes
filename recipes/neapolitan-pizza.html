Neapolitan Pizza

Description

Most famous Pizza in the world from the city 'Naples" where Pizza was invented

Note : This recipe requires a Pizza Oven which can reach upto 800°F

Ingredients

  • flour
  • water
  • salt
  • yeast
  • semolina
  • san marzano tomatoes
  • fresh mozzarella
  • basil
  • extra virgin olive oil

Steps(24-36 hrs)

  1. Add 600 ml water and 1 gm yeast into a bowl
  2. Slowly add one kg flour and mix it with hands
  3. Add 30 gm salt
  4. Knead the dough for 15-20 minutes, add flour and water as per requirement
  5. Once the dough is formed, cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 2 hours
  6. Cut the dough into 4 balls, and let it proof in the refrigerator for 24 hours
  7. After proofing, take out the balls out and let them rest for 3-4 hours
  8. Crush the tomatoes and season it accordingly
  9. Preheat the oven till it reaches 800°F
  10. Sprinkle some semolina on the working station
  11. Stretch and shape the dough ball into the shape of a pizza
  12. Spread tomato sauce on the pizza
  13. Put mozzarella chunks on the pizza
  14. Put few basil leaves and drizzle extra virgin olive oil on pizza
  15. Transfer the pizza into the oven
  16. Rotate the Pizza halfway through approx. 40-50 seconds
  17. Cook for another 40 seconds
  18. Take it out of the oven and let it rest for 1 or 2 mins
  19. Cut it into pieces and enjoy
